3 Tarts

Branding project 1

Project Scope

For this project, I redesigned the logo for 3-Tarts, a well-known bakery in Ottawa. I did all the research, starting with understanding what 3-Tarts stands for and what their customers love about them. I also studied other successful bakery logos to see what works well. Using all this information, created a new logo that better represents the quality of their delicious baked goods and the friendly, welcoming atmosphere of the bakery. The new design is fresh, eye-catching, and helps to attract more customers to 3-Tarts.

Goal

The goal of this project was to create a logo that embodies the essence of 3-Tarts Bakery. I aimed to design a logo that is modern, appealing, and reflective of the bakery's high-quality products and inviting atmosphere. The new logo needed to attract new customers while retaining the charm that loyal customers love.

Planning

The planning phase of this project was essential for its success. I began by conducting a comprehensive analysis of 3-Tarts current brand identity and its competitors. This involved researching their market position, understanding their target audience, and gathering insights on brand perception. With this information, I brainstormed and sketched multiple logo concepts, considering various design elements such as color schemes, typography, and icons. Through this iterative process of refinement and feedback, I was able to select the most promising designs for further development.

Process

The process of redesigning the 3-Tarts logo involved several steps. First, I researched current design trends and gathered inspiration. Next, I brainstormed and sketched various logo ideas, focusing on colors, fonts, and symbols that represent the bakery. After choosing the best sketches, I created digital versions and refined them through several iterations. Each version was reviewed and adjusted to make sure it captured the bakery's essence. Finally, I selected the best design that perfectly represents 3-Tarts Bakery.

Final Design

The final design of the 3-Tarts logo captures the bakery's spirit perfectly. It looks modern and friendly, with a simple, stylish font and a fun icon that shows their delicious baked goods. The new logo stands out and is easy to remember, matching the bakery's brand and attracting both new and loyal customers.

Divy Herbs

Branding project 2

Project Scope

This was my current project in which I was asked for designing a logo and business card for a herbal business in India named Divy Herbs, a reputable supplier of Ayurvedic raw materials and herbs. The objective is to create a visual identity that reflects the brand's commitment to natural, high-quality products. The logo should embody the essence of Ayurveda, combining traditional elements with a modern aesthetic. The business card design will ensure consistent branding, providing a professional and memorable first impression to clients and partners.

Goal

The goal of this project is to develop a distinctive and cohesive brand identity for Divy Herbs that resonates with their core values of natural wellness and quality. The new logo will capture the essence of Ayurveda, while the business card will reinforce this identity, ensuring it is professional, memorable, and visually appealing. This branding will help Divy Herbs stand out in the market and build trust with their clients and partners.

Planning

In the planning phase, I researched Ayurvedic branding and current design trends in the herbal industry. I analyzed Divy Herbs' market position, target audience, and competitors. Based on this, I sketched logo concepts that blend traditional Ayurvedic elements with modern design. For the business card, I explored layouts and typography to match the new logo and brand image. This planning ensured a cohesive and impactful brand identity for Divy Herbs.

Process

The process started with brainstorming and sketching various logo ideas. I then selected the best sketches and created digital versions using design software. After refining these designs, I gathered feedback and made necessary adjustments to ensure the logo truly represented Divy Herbs. Once the logo was finalized, I designed the business card, making sure it included the new logo and had a professional, cohesive look. Each step was carefully taken to ensure the final designs were effective and aligned with the brand’s identity.

Final Design

The final design features a modern yet traditional logo that perfectly captures the essence of Divy Herbs. The elegant font and natural elements in the logo convey the brand's dedication to Ayurvedic principles. The business card complements the logo with a clean and professional layout, ensuring a cohesive and memorable brand identity.
